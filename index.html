About Me

Hi! I'm Chun-Kai (Ken) Kao, and I am currently a masters student in Electrical Engineering at Stanford University with an emphasis on Human-Computer Interaction and Artificial Intelligence. I am interested in combining my passion in cleantech, software, and entrepreneurship.

Work

After completing my undergrad, I spent a year serving in the Republic of China Marine Corps. Prior to that, I have interned at Palantir Technologies, Opower, Facebook, and Hewlett-Packard. Want to know more? Check out my previous technical and business projects and resume.

Education

I graduated from Stanford University with a B.S. in Electrical Engineering in 2012. I also held board positions at Stanford Consulting (SC), Green Alliance for Innovative Action (GAIA), and Asia-Pacific Student Entrepreneurship Society (ASES), and was a member of the Phi Kappa Psi fraternity.

Fun Fact
